Welcome to Fractex

This is the ultimate fractal-explorer based on WebGL! You can create fractals in your own browser and explore them live. All fractal-explorers have the ability to be changed by parameters, so you can see, how different they look with other options.

Fractex has a responsible design so it's possible to use these explorers also on a smartphone or tablet. No need of an app, because all you need is in your browser! This site was build as lightweight as possible to give you the best experiences on the go.
