Welcome to Fractex

This is the ultimate fractal explorer based on WebGL! You can create fractals in your own browser and explorer them, live. All fractals have the ability to be changed by parameters, so you can see, how they may look differently with other options.

In the moment this project is under development, but you can explore some fractals yet. Please see the list below to test one of my fractal explorers.
